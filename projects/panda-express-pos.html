Overview

A React-based point-of-sale and kiosk system designed around how a real fast-casual line moves: fast item selection, combo logic, and separate interfaces for an employee-facing register and a customer-facing self-order kiosk.

PostgreSQL handles menu data, live orders, and order history behind the scenes, with the goal of reducing the time between a customer placing an order and that order reaching the kitchen.
